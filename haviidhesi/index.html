This is my Work From Home and My Gaming set up

Try clicking the on the image for more information!

I have been working from home since June due to Covid. I work as a Client Adviser for a bank. I work up to 8-10 hours a day and once I clock off work I tend to start gaming so therefore I do spend majority of my time on my desk.

I absolutely adore my set up. I wanted a set up that had studio vibes, with dash of RGB, yet having a minimalist look and I feel like I have achieved that. Having the perfect desk set up is important to me as it makes you feel motivated to get up everyday, start work and get things done.

Breakdown of my equipment

My Desk: IKEA

I decided to go with the popular Ikea battlestation like many others. I’m rocking Grey Alex Drawer on right and Black Trestle Leg on the left with Karlby Oak/Veener 74” (186cm) counter top. I love this counter top, can not recommend it enough to anyone.

My PC: Built myself

All parts purchased mWAVE.com.au and GPU from PLE.com.au

CPU: Ryzen 7 3700x

Motherboard: B450 Pro Max Tomahawk

GPU: Zotac RTX 3080 AMP HOLO

PSU: Cooler master 750W 80+ Gold Fully Modular

RAM: Corsair 16GB 3600Mhz

Case: Cooler Master TD500

I love to utilise my pc to its full potential as it allows me to max out any game in 1440p or 4K in max settings. Besides gaming and work my PC is used for photo editing, music, typing and casual browsing on reddit of course.

Keyboard: Keychron K4

I love this keyboard as it gives me the flexibility to go wireless to avoid cable clutter and over all it is a great typing experience. Recently changed from Tactile Blue to Gateron Brown switches (blue switches are too tactile for long hours for work).

Mouse: Logitech G305

Once again, I wanted a wireless mouse to keep the minimal look. G305 did not disappoint, great for everyday use especially for gaming.

Speakers: Creative Pebble v2

Great speakers, super affordable and quality is amazing for the price and they look great.

Monitor: Xiaomi Mi 34” Ultrawide

This monitor is amazing as its budget friendly and allows you to game at 1440p at 144hz. Not only it’s a great gaming monitor, the 21:9 aspect ratio allows you to multitask and navigate easily. I upgraded from dual monitor set up and I have not looked back since.

Lamp: LifeSmart Cololights

One of my favourite purchases. Not only does the Cololight lamp fill out the RGB aspect of my set up, it looks like an art deco when it’s turned off. Super easy to control via Cololight app. Definitely recommend this product.
