Working From My Room

I love my little work space. It's quite small but it already has everything I need and I never find myself struggling for a place to put things. I don't spend much time at my desk so it's okay that it's in my bedroom too.

As time goes on and my workload increases, I will probably start looking for somewhere else to get set up. It's nice to seperate work space from sleeping space sometimes. I wouldn't want to feel like I spend my whole life in my room haha.

I'm a web developer and most of my work is done in the office. I prefer to work in the office because we collaborate in a small team a lot right now and it's covenient to just call across the office to discuss ideas. Zoom is okay but interaction is a bit too lacking for me.

That's an example of my work. It has been one of my favourite projects so far. The pacing was just right and we had a lot of freedom to try things that we normally wouldn't, which the client loved.

Breakdown of my equipment

I'll take you through all the major parts of my setup and why I have them...

Macbook Pro

I got the smaller screen size and went with this one over the air because I wanted to keep it for a few years without needing to upgrade for a while. It's a few years old now so definitely not brand new but I love it and it still does everything I want it to. I've been struggling for storage recently but that's my own fault. I should really delete the files I don't need anymore or archive them somewhere.

Desk

I needed a desk to fit in my room without taking up too much space. I didn't want to waste too much time looking for one so I measured the space and almost picked the first white desk I found with the right dimensions. There isn't much to it and it does the job. In hind sight, I probably should have spent more time looking for one because I've sat at some pretty bad desks in my time but I got lucky I guess.

Lamp

The window is on the other side of the room which is a little frustrating but I miss out on blinding sunlight in my eyes at certain parts of the day. The lamp is to fill in the gaps when I need it. It gives some ambient light to hopefully protect my eyes from staring at a bright screen when it gets a bit darker. I don't use it much but it's nice decoration. I'm minimalist but the desk would look a little too barren without it.

Artwork

A little plug for my own work here. I'm technical for work and have a little side hobby I use to unwind. I make artwork. I've always been creative and now that I've got a bit more time on my hands, I'd like to pursue my artwork a bit more. I haven't found my niche yet so there's a bit of everything, I'm working on it. Check it out and buy a print if you like anything.
